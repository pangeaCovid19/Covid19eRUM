Pangea Formazione



Pangea Formazione is a company which focuses mainly on Data Science supporting management, through the application of scientifical research methods to corporate issues. Its team has a strong analytical background; more than 80% of its staff has obtained a PhD in scientific disciplines. Pangea's strongest feature is the ability to apply the most advanced scientific research methodologies in order to solve company issues.

In addition to having obtained UNI EN ISO 9001:2015 certification for IT software design and development for predictive model managing, Pangea Formazione has been acknowledged by the Italian Ministry of Education, University and Research as an authorized Research Institute. Moreover, Pangea stands among the companies which possess all requirements to constitute the Industry 4.0 Competence Centre, together with the University of Naples "Federico II".
